      Download HOIA mobile app

      With the HOIA mobile application, you can quickly find out about possible close contact with a COVID-19 infected person. If you have reported yourself ill in the application, you will be able to anonymously notify people who have been in contact with you and prevent the virus from spreading.The application cannot access the data on your phone or know your location. Neither the country nor anyone else can see what's happening in your mobile app.

      When you start confirming your infection, the application will redirect you to the Patient Portal. The Patient Portal does not return your health information to the application, but only confirms the fact of infection to the server in a non-personalized form. After that, any close contacts will receive notifications on their phone through the HOIA application.
      Read more: www.hoia.me

      Medical invoices submitted to the Estonian Health Insurance Fund

      Here you can view the medical invoices submitted by health care institutions to the Estonian Health Insurance Fund in relation to your treatment. All the invoices presented here are paid by the Estonian Health Insurance Fund from the resources of the health insurance collected as part of social tax (13% of the social tax) that the employers withhold from the monthly salary of employees. The Estonian health insurance system follows the principle of solidarity: in the case of illness, the cost of the health care service paid by the Health Insurance Fund does not depend on the amount of social tax paid for each person. The Health Insurance Fund shall use the social tax paid for employed persons to pay for the health insurance services of those persons who are not employed at the moment. If necessary, the health insurance component resources are used to pay for your and other insured persons’ various health-related monetary expenses (medicinal products, medicinal aids, compensation for temporary incapacity for work etc.) in addition to medical invoices. Additional information about health insurance, medical invoices and your insurance status can be found on the homepage of the Estonian Health Insurance Fund at www.haigekassa.ee.

      Managing accesses to health data

      Individuals are able to grant or withdraw their consent allowing third parties to access their health data by selecting an appropriate option from the page of access to health data of the state´s Patient Portal. Third parties are institutions that have the legal right to use data, only within specified extent and purpose, contained in the health information system. In order to process data, third parties need an additional consent of the person.

      In Patient Portal, one can manage consentsgranting medical commissions and medical peer review commission of the Ministry of Defence access to data. These medical commissions have the legal right to access health data in the health information system if the person has given their consent. The main task of medical commissions consisting of medical specialists is to assess the conformity of the health condition of persons liable to or wishing to undertake national defence obligation to requirements established for servicemen. If the person does not grant prior electronic consent in the Patient Portal, they must adhere to the former data collection process, and provide all required documents describing the health condition to the medical commission on paper.

      Information about notifications

      At the moment, you can receive notifications regarding screening invitations and health certificates from the documents at the national patient portal.

      You will recieve notifications about your health certificates to:
      • the patient portal (www.digilugu.ee)
      • the state portal (www.eesti.ee)
      • the e-mail address you use daily

      You will receive notifications to the e-mail address you use daily if you have redirected your state portal e-mails to the e-mail address you use daily. You can set up your e-mail address at here. Additional information can be found at here. You will receive notifications upon the decision to issue a health certificate, when updates to a health certificate are made, if a health certificate is about to expire (advance notice 30 days prior to expiry) and when it expires.
