      In order to check the result of the coronavirus SARS-CoV-2 test, click on the referral response box (Saatekirja vastus) below.

      In case of a negative test result: Wash your hands as often as possible and avoid close contacts to prevent infection. Please note, that a negative test result is not going to prevent the possibility of a later infection.If you experience any symptoms, stay home immediately and contact your family doctor. During the weekends, the family doctors advice line, 1220, will assist you with the testing. In addition, download HOIA mobile app, which alerts you, in case you’ve been in close contact with a COVID-19 positive person.

      In case of a positive test result: Stay home! The Health Board will contact you. Call your family doctor and inform them that your SARS-CoV-2 test came back positive. Your family doctor will issue you a sick leave in case you need one. If you use HOIA mobile app, mark yourself as sick in the application. That way the people you’ve been in close contact will get an early warning and can stay in self isolation. If you feel unwell, contact your family doctor or call 112 immediately.
